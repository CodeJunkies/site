We Build Web Things

What makes a Developer "Great"? Some would argue that practice makes perfect. But when it comes to programming, be it Web, Native, or Core, we would say that experience plays a major factor into making a Developer "Great."

Building experience is what we're all about. Our industry grows by sharing our knowledge from one developer to another. In fact, that's the true beauty of what we do. If we don't know how to code something, there's a good chance that someone out there does, and has most likely shared how they did on their blog or in a forum. It's what we do, it's how we get recognition, and it's how we continue to keep building smarter, faster, and cleaner apps.

The Code Junkies is more than just another Meetup group that hosts a 45-minute keynote about a technology once a month. Our goal is to build a community of developers that can get together on their own terms, based on shared interests, and possibly collaborate on app ideas. Rather than focus on a single programming language, framework, or technology, we prefer to encourage them all, and focus our energy on producing events that encourage our members to think out of the box, and build something great.

Over the next few months we will be establishing some strategic partnerships, launching a new online community, and participating in some area coding challenges. Sol if you haven't done so already, please join our new online portal "The Developer's Lounge"
